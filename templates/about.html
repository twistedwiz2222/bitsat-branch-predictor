About BITSAT Branch Predictor

Branch Cutoffs

The branch cutoffs displayed in this predictor are based on the 2024 BITSAT admission data. These cutoffs represent the minimum score required to be eligible for each branch at respective campuses.

Note that these are the actual cutoffs from 2024, and they may vary in future years based on:

  • Number of applicants
  • Overall performance of test-takers
  • Available seats in each branch
How to Use the Predictor

Simply enter your BITSAT score in the input field on the home page and click the "Find My Eligible Branches" button. The predictor will display all branches across all three BITS campuses (Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad) for which you would have been eligible based on the 2024 cutoffs.

The branches are sorted by cutoff score, with the most competitive branches (highest cutoffs) appearing first.

Important Note: This predictor is meant to be used as a guidance tool based on historical data. The actual cutoffs for future years may vary from the 2024 values shown here.
